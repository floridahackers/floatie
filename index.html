A rising tide lifts all boats

So how do we rise?
Bring all of the collegiate tech community organizaers into one space and see what we can do. Enter floatie.
Floatie is a weekend long conference for those students obsessed with using the collegiate platform to create a culture or community and collaboration in tech at their own Florida universities. The goal of this conference is to connect student leaders across our Sunshine State in a movement to break university boundaries, work as a state rather than a school to convert Florida into a state fostering and cultivating the next generation to change the state of tech.

Where is Floatie?

Floatie will be held at the historic Church Street Exchange , located in the heart of beautiful Orlando, Florida.
Throughout the weekend, we will be holding events in the Main Atrium, as well as Canvs (a coworking space) and the Orlando Iron Yard . For housing during Saturday night, we will be staying at villas in the beautiful Championsgate Resort, near the Disney Resort area.
