Who is speaking at this conference?

You are!

Floatie is about sharing your ideas, so what better way to share your brilliant ideas than on a fully decked out stage?

Apply to become a speaker. Tell us what you have to say, how you hope to influence, and in what setting you would like to speak. Our organizing team will work with you to make sure your talk is polished and can have the greatest impact possible. Our entire event is built around our talks, so make sure to bring the thunder. We hope the talks will share incredible stories about building creative communities with lessons that our attendees can take to their universities and organizations.

So what are you waiting for? Apply to talk, share the stage with some of the greatest tech leaders in Florida.
Inspire, be heard, change our state.

There are five types of talks at this conference:

Keynote

The main attraction. The Keynote talk is meant to set the tone for the conference and hold the heaviest influence.

Main Talk

Sign up: Available

The main talk is our rudder. Set on our main stage with all attendees, this slot is anyone’s opportunity to speak to all of Floatie and guide the weekend.

Discussion Sessions

Sign up: Available

Set in one of our beautiful group discussion areas, Discussion Sessions are where attendees can sit together and dissect a topic determined prior by a discussion leader who also guides the dialogue and helps stimulate conversation.

Lighting Discussions

Sign up: Available

Sparks discussion. Quite literally. A single lightning discussion talk slot will contain three speakers, presenting on stage for 5 minutes one-after-another, then splitting up into the discussion rooms for a 20 minute group discussion about the talk.

Barcamp Discussions

Sign up: During Event

Thought of a great idea right in the middle of Floatie? No problem! That’s what Barcamp is for. Propose a topic, then based on overall interest, we will allocate time in a discussion area. Preparation not required. Serendipity encouraged.
